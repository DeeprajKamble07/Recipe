Ingredents for Pomfret Fish Rava Fry

Ingredients: Pomfret fish, chilli powder, turmeric, garlic, rava, salt, oil, and other spices as needed.

Steps to Make Pomfret Fish Rava Fry:

Clean the Fish: Wash the pomfret thoroughly and make 2â€“3 shallow slits on both sides for the masala to seep in. Prepare the Masala: In a bowl, mix red chilli powder, turmeric powder, crushed garlic, and salt. Add a little water or lemon juice to make a thick paste. Marinate: Apply the masala paste all over the fish, including inside the slits. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (or more for better flavor). Coat with Rava: Take semolina (rava) on a plate. Gently coat the marinated fish evenly on both sides. Shallow Fry: Heat oil in a flat pan on medium flame. Place the coated fish and fry on both sides until golden brown and crispy. Avoid high flame to prevent burning. Serve: Remove from the pan, place on a tissue to absorb extra oil. Serve hot with onion rings and lemon wedges.
